👤 About Me

The story behind Tech Hub 2.0

Hey there! I'm Manish Aryal, an aspiring software developer and AI enthusiast from Nepal 🇳🇵. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Information Management (BIM) and I'm deeply passionate about building smart solutions that help students, learners, and creators.

Tech Hub 2.0 was born out of my mission to simplify learning in Nepal. I noticed a lack of well-organized tech resources for BIM, CSIT, Engineering, and BIT students. So, I built this platform from scratch using my skills in HTML, Python, and AI tools like GPT and OpenCV.

My core interests are:

  • 🧠 Artificial Intelligence
  • 💡 Python Projects
  • 🎮 Game Development
  • 🌐 Web & App Development
  • 📘 Educational Innovation

My goal is to turn Tech Hub 2.0 into a powerful, free learning engine for everyone. Thank you for being part of the journey!
